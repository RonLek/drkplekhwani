Dr. Kanhaiyalal. P. Lekhwani

Scholar, Lexicographer, Critic, Translator

About

Dr. Kanhaiyalal Lekhwani (born 1942, Hyderabad, Sindh) M.A. Ph.D.(linguistics); Former Associate Professor and Head, Department of Sindhi, WRLC (Central Institute of Indian Languages, Ministry of HRD, Government of India) Deccan College, Pune. He has been engaged in research, training, designing instructional material and actively organising conferences, academic seminars, expert workshops, language policy meetings.

Over the past four decades, Dr. Lekhwani has conducted and organised several Sindhi language and literature related orientation programs.

Worked as a Joint Secretary, Linguistic Society of India; Member, Maharashtra Rajya Sindhi Sahitya Academy, Mumbai ; Member, the Curriculum Committee for Secondary School Level Course in Sindhi, National Institute of Open Schooling, New Delhi; Expert Member , Sindhi Language Teaching Progamme, National Council For Promotion of Sindhi Language, New Delhi; Member, Expert Committee Sindhi Language and Culture Centre, IGNOU, New Delhi; Member, Sindhi Advisory Board, Sahitya Akademi, New Delhi and Member, Advisory Panel for Sindhi, National Book Trust - India, New Delhi

Received many National and International Awards, Dr. Lekhwani continues contributing in the field of Sindhi Language, Literature and Education.

Panhinji Boli - Sindhi(Level I) CIIL - KVS Mother Tongue Series (1987)

Set of three books: Text Book, Copy Book, Teachers' Manual. Published in both scripts: Devanagiri Sindhi and Arabic Sindhi; CIIL Mysore

An Intensive Course In Sindhi (1987)

Specially prepared for second language learners, having 72 lessons with English meaning and grammatical notes in English.(Pages - XXI -882). CIIL Mysore

Sindhiyat Ji Sugandh (An Intermediate Course in Sindhi) (1999)

Having 30 lessons with exercises. Most of the lessons are based on topics related to Sindhi Culture. Published in both scripts: Devanagiri Sindhi(Pages - XII + 143) and Arabic Sindhi(Pages - XII + 152). CIIL Mysore

Adabi Guldasto (An Advanced Course in Sindhi) (1999)

30 selected literary pieces with exercises. Published in both scripts: Devanagiri Sindhi(Pages: XII + 211) and Arabic Sindhi(Pages: XII + 223). CIIL Mysore

Sindhi Boli Ain Adab Ji Tarikh (History of Sindhi Language And Literature) (2011)

A brief history of Sindhi language and literature upto 2008 is given in this book. Published in both scripts: Devanagiri Sindhi(Pages: 398) and Arabic Sindhi(Pages: 397). CIIL Mysore.

Sindhi Sahit Jo Mukhtasar Itihas (A Brief History of Sindhi Literature from the beginning to 2015) (2016)

Published in both sripts: Devanagiri Sindhi(Pages : 398) and Arabic Sindhi(Pages : 397). Lekhwani Publication, Pune.

Sindhi Newspaper Reader (2008)

Supplementary Reader for foreigners learning Sindhi. Selected, graded Sindhi news items with English translation and exercises are given in the book. Indian Institute of Language Studies, Dunwood Press, MD 70782 USA

Tests of Language Proficiency - Sindhi (2007)

Evaluation and testing of Sindhi Language upto X Standard, prepared along with other scholars. Published in both scripts: Devanagiri Sindhi(Pages: XII + 235) and Arabic Sindhi(Pages: XII + 231). CIIL Mysore.

Basic Sindhi - Hindi Dictionary (1995)

About 12000 lexical items in Sindhi in both scripts (Devanagiri and Arabic) with Hindi meanings are given in this book. (Pages: VIII + 162). Prakash Publications, Pune

Basic Sindhi - English Dictionary (2001)

About 13000 lexical items in Sindhi in both scripts (Devanagiri and Arabic and Roman transliteration) with English meanings are given in this book.(Pages: 194) Prakash Publications, Pune.

Basic Sindhi - English - Hindi Dictionary (1996)

About 13000 lexical items in Sindhi in both scripts (Devanagiri and Arabic and Roman transliteration) with English and Hindi meanings are given in this book.(Pages: 194) Prakash Publications, Pune.

Sindhi - Hindi - English Pictorial Dictionary (1998)

Sindhi words (in both scripts) with Hindi and English meanings with pictures in 64 classified chapters are given in this book.(Pages: IV + 64). Prakash Publications, Pune.

Pictorial Glossary in Sindhi (2011)

Sindhi lexical items in both scripts are given with pictures. (Pages: XII + 136). CIIL Mysore.

A Glossary of English - Sindhi Technical Terms (Grammar) (2017)

Sindhi grammatical technical terms used upto higher secondary level are given in English with Sindhi meanings in both scripts. (Pages: 128). Lekhwani Publications, Pune.

A Practical Sindhi - Hindi Dictionary (2005)

About 16000 Sindhi lexical items in both sripts(Devanagiri Sindhi and Arabic Sindhi) with Hindi meanings are given. Published by Kanhaiyalal Lekhwani, Pune. Its 3rd edition is published by Sindhi Academy Delhi in 2011.(Pages: XXII + 441)

A Practical Sindhi - English Dictionary (2014)

About 17000 lexical items in both scripts (Devanagiri Sindhi and Arabic Sindhi) with English meanings are given. (Pages: VI + 723). Published by National Council for Promotion of Sindhi Language(NCPSL), New Delhi.

Sindhi Shabda-Roop(Sindhi Word - Forms) (2014)

About 13000 basic lexical items with their inflectional forms in both scrips(Devanagiri Sindhi and Arabic Sindhi) are given in the book. Compiled along with Dr. Bhojraj Lekhwani. (Pages: XVIII + 551). Published By: Chhattisgarh Sindhi Sahitya Sansthan (Akademy), Raipur.

Bharat Mein Sindhi Talim (Sindhi Education in India - A Survey) (1997)

Conducted the survey of Sindhi Education in India. Its report has been published in book form by Sindhi Academy, Delhi. (Pages: X + 119)

Rashtriya Abhyaskram Rooprekha (2005)

Sindhi translations of National Curriculum Framework - 2005 is given. (Pages: XXI +164). Published by NCERT, New Delhi.

Virhange Joon Choond Sindhi Kahaniyoon (2017)

24 selected Sindhi short stories on the Partition written by Indian Sindhi authors are given in this book. Published by National Book Trust- India, New Delhi.
